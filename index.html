How It Works

In three simple steps, a fully automated, on-chain process.

01

Creator Reward Collection

The pump.fun token creator reward is continuously accumulated in the project wallet after each trade.

02

Holder Snapshot

The system takes a snapshot of the targeted token holders at regular intervals, proportional to the amount they hold.

03

Automatic Airdrop

The accumulated SOL is automatically distributed in batch transactions directly to the holders' wallets.
